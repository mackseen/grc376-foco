About Bloom

Discover the delightful fusion of Asian flavors at Bloom, a cozy local cafe and bakery in San Luis Obispo.

Founded by a group of Cal Poly students seeking more diverse culinary options, Bloom has become a beloved spot for both students and locals.

Our passionate team of baristas and bakers crafts a variety of freshly baked pastries, cakes, and breads, featuring unique ingredients like matcha, black sesame, yuzu, and red bean. Complement your treat with our specialty coffees, teas, and refreshing beverages.

Come experience the warmth and creativity of Bloom!
